Konstantinos in (1) paragraph

Ι was born in Athens, Greece, in 1985. I studied at the Athens University of Economic and Business - AUEB - and the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens - UOA from which I obtained a bachelor's degree in Statistics, as well as a master's degree in Biostatistics. My master thesis in UOA was performed under the supervision of Ioannis Ntzoufras on Bayesian variable selection in Normal and Binomial models with application in Medical research. I conducted my PhD researching statistical methods for the synthesis of rare diseases data within the Asterix project (EU-FP7 2013-2017). I became affiliated for 2 years with the Hellenic Center of Disease and Control Prevention center where I focused among other taks on resetting the National Public health registry. I have worked in or collaborated with university hospitals both in Greece, The Netherlands, Germany and the United Kingdom. I am currently affiliated with the the University Medical Center Utrecht and the University of Thessaly.
